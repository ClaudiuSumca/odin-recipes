Easy Overnight Oats

How to Make Overnight Oats With Banana

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Easy Overnight Oats Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats
  • Milk
  • Banana
  • Honey
  • Cinnamon

Steps to follow:

  1. Combine all the ingredients (besides the banana) in a jar, seal, and shake.
  2. Open the jar and fold in the banana chopped.
  3. Seal the jar and refrigerate overnight.
